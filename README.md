## üéØ Recommendations

1. **Retention & Engagement:**  
   - Focus on younger and single employees through mentorship and recognition.  
   - Conduct stay interviews to understand motivators.

2. **Enhance Job Satisfaction:**  
   - Offer role rotation and skill-building opportunities.  
   - Recognize and reward consistently.

3. **Optimize Departmental Policies:**  
   - Review workload and incentives in **R&D** and **Sales**.  
   - Encourage interdepartmental collaboration.

4. **Promote Work-Life Balance:**  
   - Introduce flexible schedules and wellness programs.  
   - Train managers to monitor burnout indicators.

5. **Reassess Compensation:**  
   - Align pay with contribution and performance.  
   - Clarify promotion pathways.

6. **Use Data-Driven HR Strategy:**  
   - Maintain a live dashboard to monitor satisfaction and retention.  
   - Use analytics to anticipate attrition hotspots.
